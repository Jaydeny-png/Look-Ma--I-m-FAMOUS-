Beethoveen

Ludwig van Beethoven was born in Bonn, Germany, in 1770. He was the eldest of three children, and his father was a court musician. Beethoven's family was not particularly wealthy, but his father recognized his musical talent at a young age and began teaching him piano and violin. Beethoven's father was a strict teacher, and he often beat his son when he did not practice enough. Despite this, Beethoven was a prodigy and began performing in public at the age of seven.
